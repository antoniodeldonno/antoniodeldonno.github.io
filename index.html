About Me

Hi, I'm Antonio Del Donno! Some words about me: I initially pursued theoretical physics, specializing in quantum field theory and beyond the standard model physics. I found an increasing fascination for the mathematical foundations of these theories and for mathematical methods in physics in general. This led me to delve into the realms of geometry and topology. Motivated by this growing passion, I enrolled in a master's degree program in pure math, where I started to comprehend I would rather become a researcher in mathematics. My second master's thesis is about noncommutative geometry. I explored the topic of differential calculi on quantum principal bundles. I am now a PhD candidate at the Charles University of Prague, with focus on the noncommutative geometry of quantum flag manifolds. Beside my main academic commitments I also serve as a tutor for the University of Bologna, specifically for the engineering and chemistry departments. Download my CV.

Music
